Consulting and Rates

Embedded Systems is a wide field with an overwhelming amount of choice. Austen is available to book for private consulting sessions. These sessions can be conducted by Phone, Skype, Google Hangouts/Google Meet or other video or audio software. An initial consult is required to ensure that his expertise is a good fit for your set of problems. All sessions are billed hourly, rounded up to the nearest hour. His rates are listed below:

  • Scoping Consult $200/hour
    - An initial consult to scope your project and problems or ask any questions
  • Focus Session $300/hour
    - Specific project guidance, solution proposals, review of work results, manufacturing and testing concerns
  • Design Session $500/hour
    - Solution implementation, part selection, full-design, fab-ready solutions, firmware deliverables
  • Complete Solution $20,000
    - Project Designed and Executed to your specifications, board design and firmware deliverable
  • On-Site Session $4000/day + travel expenses
    - An in person visit, solving your problems in physical space
